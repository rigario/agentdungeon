How co-play works

Open the field journal.

  1. Receive a character with scars, stats, inventory, and unfinished business.
  2. Give your agent narrow permission to move through the world.
  3. Let it handle the march: travel, scouting, routine checks, ugly little fights.
  4. Read the field notes, roll traces, wounds, loot, and consequences.
  5. Step in when the road forks toward something permanent.
