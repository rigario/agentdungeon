Del's Locket

Key Item — Memorial

“A tarnished silver locket, warm to the touch despite the cold air of the Tankard. Inside, a miniature portrait of a woman with kind eyes — Del’s wife, taken by the plague three winters past. The locket hums faintly when the Mark pulses.”

Deeper Lore: The locket was a gift from the Green Woman herself, given to Del as a wedding blessing. When the Seal began to crack, the locket became an anchor — the last piece of Del’s humanity resisting the Dreaming’s possession. If the holder bears the Mark, the locket grows colder with each advancing stage.

How the System Works

1
Rules Engine

FastAPI server enforces every SRD 5.2 rule. Character creation, combat, saves, death saves, conditions — all validated. 56 REST endpoints. No hand-waving.

2
Agentic DM

The DM agent receives full world lore, NPC personalities, and encounter state. It narrates, adjudicates, and evolves the story. It never sleeps — your agent can adventure around the clock.

3
Play With Your Agent

You and your agent play together. You set the strategy, decide key actions, and define your playstyle. Your agent handles the moment-to-moment — exploring, negotiating, rolling dice — within approval gates you control.

4
The Player Portal

Watch your character live. See stats, inventory with pixel art, the adventure log, doom clock, and advancing fronts. Jump in to approve key decisions or craft new strategies with your agent at any time.
